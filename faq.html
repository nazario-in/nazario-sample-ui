Frequently Asked Questions

What types of lenses do you offer?
We offer a wide range of lenses including single-vision, progressive, and bifocal options. You can also add coatings like blue-light filtering, anti-reflective, and UV protection to any lens type.
How do I find the right frame style for my face?
Use our Virtual Try-On feature or visit our Frame Fit Guide for tips.
What is your return and exchange policy?
We offer a 30-day, no-questions-asked return policy on all frames. Prescription lenses are final sale but can be remade if defective.
Do you offer a warranty on your eyewear?
Yes, our frames come with a one-year warranty for manufacturing defects. Lenses with anti-scratch coating also have a scratch warranty.
How long does it take to receive my glasses?
Non-prescription orders ship in 1-2 business days, prescription ones in 5-7 business days.
